Rinker Fiesta Vee 340 – The Perfect Blend of Luxury and Performance on the Water

Experience an unparalleled boating adventure with the Rinker Fiesta Vee 340, an elegant cruiser that seamlessly combines top-tier performance and comfort on every journey. With a length of 11 meters and a beam of 3.40 meters, this vessel offers exceptional stability and spaciousness—perfect for a relaxing voyage with up to 9 passengers, including a skipper.

The spacious interior cabin is equipped with a bed and a bathroom featuring a shower, ensuring all the comfort you need for an enjoyable stay on the water. Whether you're planning a weekend getaway or an extended cruise, the Rinker Fiesta Vee 340 allows you to unwind and savor every moment.

Powered by twin Mercruiser engines with 5.7L displacement and 250 horsepower each, this boat delivers outstanding performance, allowing you to achieve speed, efficiency, and stability in any conditions.

More than just a boat, the Rinker Fiesta Vee 340 is your gateway to relaxation and adventure. Whether you want to explore serene coastlines or embrace the thrill of high-speed cruising, this yacht is the perfect choice for your next boating adventure.
