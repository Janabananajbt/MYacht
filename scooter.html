Yamaha GP 1300R – High-Performance Jet Ski for True Speed Enthusiasts

Unleash the thrill of water sports with the legendary Yamaha GP 1300R, a jet ski engineered for those who crave adrenaline and precision. With its aggressive design and lightweight hull, the GP 1300R cuts through the water with unmatched agility and power.

At the heart of this machine lies a 1,298cc, 2-stroke engine with fuel injection, designed to deliver blistering acceleration and incredible top speeds. Whether you're racing across open waters or carving tight turns, the GP 1300R responds with instant throttle and razor-sharp control.

Thanks to its compact design and ergonomic seating, this jet ski provides maximum comfort and maneuverability for solo riders or two passengers. The reinforced hull ensures superior handling, even in choppy conditions, making every ride smooth and exhilarating.

The Yamaha GP 1300R isn’t just a jet ski—it’s a high-performance watercraft built for pure fun, unmatched performance, and total freedom on the water. Whether you're a seasoned rider or stepping into the world of jet skiing, the GP 1300R is your ultimate companion for speed and adventure.
